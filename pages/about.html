AboutUs

Founded in the vibrant city of Los Angeles, CA, in late 2023, Kitty Sitty is a trailblazer in the world of cat care. Our mission is simple yet vital: to provide top-notch care for cats while their owners are away. We understand that cats are more than pets; they're valued family members, and we treat them with the love and respect they deserve.


At the core of our service is a robust platform that connects cat enthusiasts with those in need of cat-sitting services. This platform enables us to create a network of skilled and passionate cat sitters who offer personalized, in-home care. Our cat sitters are carefully selected and trained in the latest feline care practices, ensuring a safe and nurturing environment for your feline companions.


Whether you're traveling or simply need temporary cat care, Kitty Sitty is here to offer peace of mind and exceptional service. Join the Kitty Sitty family, where every cat is treated as a Very Important Pet.
